Scope and terms

Scroll to read in full

1. What's included in setup

For the agreed setup fee, we will design, build, and launch one website including all pages and features outlined in the proposal. This includes one round of revisions before launch.

2. Monthly care plan (Option B only)

The care plan is optional and not required to purchase the website. If you choose Option B, $75/month covers: hosting and SSL, routine software and security updates, uptime monitoring, and up to 30 minutes of minor content edits per month (e.g. updating text, prices, hours, swapping a photo). Unused time does not roll over.

3. What's not included (billed separately)

The following are outside the care plan and quoted and billed separately: new pages or sections, design changes or redesigns, new features (booking systems, online stores, etc.), content edits beyond 30 minutes per month, and copywriting or photography services. We'll always quote extra work before starting it, nothing is billed without your approval.

4. Handover (Option A)

If you choose Option A, at launch we'll provide hosting and domain access details (or transfer the site to a hosting account in your name), and brief written instructions for basic upkeep. From handover, you're responsible for hosting, security, uptime, and future changes. You're welcome to take up the care plan later, or request one-off paid work at our standard hourly rate, subject to availability.

5. Turnaround and edit requests (Option B)

Routine edit requests are completed within 2 to 3 business days. Requests are batched and processed on set days each week. Urgent requests (broken links, factual errors) are prioritised where possible. Send requests by email with a clear description so there's a written record for both of us.

6. Payment terms

  • Setup fee: due before work begins on final launch
  • Monthly care plan (if chosen): billed monthly in advance, due within 7 days of invoice
  • Late payments may result in the site being taken offline until payment is received, with prior notice

7. Cancellation (Option B)

Either party may cancel the care plan with 30 days' written notice. Upon cancellation, Section 4 (Handover) applies.

8. Ownership

Once the setup fee is paid in full, the website design and content created for you are yours. The underlying code template and reusable components remain our intellectual property.

9. Your responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate, lawful content (text, images, logos, reviews) that you have the right to use, and to respond to requests for content or approval in a timely manner.
